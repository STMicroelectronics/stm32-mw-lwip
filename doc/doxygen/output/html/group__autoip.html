Detailed Description

AUTOIP related functions USAGE:

define LWIP_AUTOIP 1 in your lwipopts.h

Without DHCP:

  • Call autoip_start() after netif_add().

With DHCP:

  • define LWIP_DHCP_AUTOIP_COOP 1 in your lwipopts.h.
  • Configure your DHCP Client.
